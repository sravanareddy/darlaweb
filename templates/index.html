About

DARLA is designed and implemented by Sravana Reddy and James Stanford.

Our system makes use of automatic speech recognition using the Google Speech API, FAVE-Extract, ProsodyLab-Aligner, and the R Vowels package to automate vowel analysis for sociophonetics.
